| 2-Year Yield Zone    | What It Usually Means         | Market Effect      |
| -------------------- | ----------------------------- | ------------------ |
| **Below 2.5%**       | Easy policy                   | Bullish for stocks |
| **2.5–4%**           | Neutral / normalizing         | Mixed              |
| **4–5%**             | Tight policy                  | Stocks at risk     |
| **>5%**              | Very tight / recession signal | High market stress |
| **> Fed funds rate** | Fed behind the curve          | Stocks vulnerable  |
| **>10-year yield**   | Curve inversion               | Recession risk ↑   |

| 10-Year Yield Level | What It Usually Signals     | Stock Market Impact |
| ------------------- | --------------------------- | ------------------- |
| **<3%**             | Accommodative conditions    | Bullish             |
| **3–4%**            | Neutral/normal              | Mixed               |
| **4–5%**            | Tightening, stress building | Elevated risk       |
| **>5%**             | Very tight, recession risk  | High market stress  |
